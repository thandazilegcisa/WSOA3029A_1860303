About:

This project aims to use the Air Quality Open Data Api to examine and objectively determine which places around the world have the best or worst air quality. As such the core aim of this is to tell a story as to the potential reasons why a city or place may or may not have good air quality. Consequently this also drives the narrative that the world is changing and we (humans) are catalysts or agents of that change either good or bad.


The main point of difference that will be used to derive this narrative includes assimilating the air quality index of a given city. Using the D3.js library. I plan on working more on the creative component of the project now that i've established a narrative and have my requests being heard.


Website Style Guide :


Considering the fact that I now have a solid understanding of HTML,CSS,javascript. And knowing that my website last semester was simplistic and did not make much use of color. I made it a point that i establish the tone, feel, colour palette and some of the experience first, before i handled all my data. So that i can focus my attention on that for the remainder of the project instead of styling, but rather the data and how a user interacts with it.
